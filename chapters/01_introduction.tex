\chapter{Introduction}

{\color{orange}
    \textit{Note: the chapter titles and overall structure are suggestions. Discuss the structure that best fits your work with your supervisor before you start writing.}
    \vspace{1em}

    Start with a motivation that outlines why your scientific endeavor is necessary and novel.
    Subsequently, elaborate the research question and derive the objective of your thesis. 
    Make sure that the introduction is coherent with the last chapter, i.e. your research question should be answered in the conclusion.
}
