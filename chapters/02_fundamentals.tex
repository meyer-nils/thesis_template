{
    \color{orange}
    This chapter could also be named \emph{literature review}, \emph{background}, \emph{research gap}, \emph{objective}, etc. 
    Adjust the name and structure to your needs.
    
    Prepare the reader with all the fundamental knowledge required to understand the following chapters. 
    While writing, assume that the reader has a professional background in engineering or materials science, but without a deep knowledge within the specific field of research. 
    Make sure to adequately present relevant scientific literature on the given topic (State of Research).
    Ideally, you should end this chapter by identifying a research gap that has not been addressed in the literature yet. 
    The objective of your thesis is then to fill this gap.
